# Sell/Trade Funnel Validation

## Goal

Validate whether the quote tool can become a repeatable inventory acquisition channel before investing heavily in paid traffic.

## Test Group

Recruit 5-10 friends, collectors, or local sellers. Send only the Sell/Trade link and ask them to act as if they found it online.

## Observe

- Did they understand that the quote is no obligation?
- Did they understand market value versus cash offer versus trade credit?
- Did they know what happens after accepting or submitting?
- Did they trust the quote enough to continue?
- What made them hesitate?
- Would they submit real cards?

## Track

- Sell/Trade page visits
- Quote starts
- Quote completions
- Offer selections
- Cash versus trade selections
- Submission completions
- Average estimated market value
- Average cash offer value
- Average trade credit value

## Current Event Names

- `sell_trade_quote_started`
- `sell_trade_quote_completed`
- `sell_trade_offer_selected`
- `sell_trade_submission_started`
- `sell_trade_submission_completed`

## Pass Criteria

The next scaling step is justified when real sellers consistently submit quotes each week and the largest repeated trust or process objections have been fixed.
